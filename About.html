Vision

King Universitywill have a transformative impact on society through continual innovation in education,technology, research, creativity, entrepreneurship and collaboration

Mission

King University mission is to create a transformative educational experience for students focused on deep disciplinary knowledge, problem solving, leadership, communication, and interpersonal skills and personal health and well-being.
To cultivate a transformative university community committed to:

  • attracting and retaining diverse, world-class talent.
  • creating a collaborative environment open to the free exchange of ideas, where research, creativity, innovation, and entrepreneurship can flourish
  • ensuring individuals can achieve their full potential.

To impact society in a transformative way regionally, nationally, and globally by engaging with partners outside the traditional borders of the king university campus.

History

The history of king university Trust marks its beginning on 22st day of June, 2018. With an objective to provide quality education and help student fraternity achieve academic excellence and brilliance in all walks of life.
